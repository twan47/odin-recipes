Fluffy Pancakes

Irresistable fluffy pancakes for the win!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • cooking spray

Steps

    Step 1
  1. Combine milk with vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to "sour"
    2. Step 2
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk egg and butter into "soured" milk. Pour flour mixture into the milk mixture and whisk until lumps are gone.
    3. Step 3
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and coat it with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the skillet, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip with spatula, and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
